An automated game of Karma played by three bots against one another, with the progress and results of the game being displayed through the terminal. 
There are three types of bots that can play against each other basic player, only plays one card at a time. Set player plays all of its cards of the same value at once, 
and Smart player that plays all of its cards of low value at once but uses its cards of high value one at a time. 

# Special Cards 

The special cards for the game are as follows

7 : a 7 or lower must be played next else pick up

2 : can be played on any card and resets the pile(any card can be played on it next)

8 : can be played on any card and is transparent(the 8 is ignored for rank comparison and the card below it is used instead)

10 : can be played on any card and burns the pile

# Additional Rules

8 reverse direction - when an 8 is played the direction of play reverses

Three 3’s burn - if a player plays exactly three 3’s the pile is burned

9 of clubs steals a card - if a player plays a 9 of clubs then they steal a random card from the next player
